Mitigation Checklist:
1.Educate stakeholders and train Employees.
2.Ask your employees and partners to sign a non-disclosure agreement.
3.Identify All Critical Data and Develop Strategies for Protecting Them
4.Identify All Endpoint Devices, Hardware and Structure Connected to Your Network.
5.Develop, Set, and Enforce Security Policies.
6.Implement strict data encryption procedures and authentication policies
7.Monitor User Activities Regularly
8.Require Employees to Use VPNs When Connecting via External Networks
9.Avoid saving unnecessary data.
10.Use anomaly detection.
11.Automate as much as possible.
12.Harden your systems.
